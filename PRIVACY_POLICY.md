# Privacy Policy for ZIP Sequence Game

**Last updated: February 2026**

## Introduction

ZIP Sequence Game ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Google Account Information**: If you choose to sign in with Google, we collect your email address and basic profile information to enable cloud save functionality.

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Game Progress**: Level completion, stars earned, and best times are stored locally on your device and optionally in the cloud if you sign in.
- **Analytics Data**: We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data such as:
  - Levels played and completed
  - Time spent in the app
  - Device type and operating system
  - General geographic location (country level)

### Advertising
We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:
- Device identifiers
- IP address
- Usage data for ad personalization

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Save and sync your game progress
- Improve game performance and user experience
- Display relevant advertisements
- Analyze usage patterns to improve the game

## Data Storage and Security

- Local game data is stored on your device
- Cloud data (if signed in) is stored securely in Google Firebase
- We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data

## Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:
- **Google Firebase** (Authentication, Firestore, Analytics, Crashlytics)
- **Google AdMob** (Advertising)
- **Google Play Services**

Each service has its own privacy policy:
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## Children's Privacy

Our game does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game is designed to be family-friendly and does not require personal information to play.
